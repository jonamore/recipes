# Chili
Takes 3 hours

```
Brown 4 lbs cubed tri-tip with 3 tsp salt and 1½ tsp white pepper. Place in stock pot on low

Add:
2 Cans Chicken Broth
1 Can El Pato Tomato Sauce
1 Small Can Spicey V-8 Juice
1 tbs Onion Powder
2 tsp Garlic Powder
2 tbs Chili Powder (Gebhardt’s)

@ 1 ½ hrs Add:
2 tbs chili powder (Gebhardt’s)
5 tsp cumin
¼ tsp msg
1 tbs tabasco sauce

@ 2 hrs Add:
1 tsp New Mexico hot chili powder
1 ½ tsp tabasco sauce
¼ tsp red jalapeno powder
1 tbs chili powder (Gebhardt’s
Salt to taste

@ 2 ½ hrs Add:
5 tsp arrowroot (mix with water to form paste)
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp brown sugar
½ tsp red jalapeno powder
½ tsp cumin
Salt to taste
```
